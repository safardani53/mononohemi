    ANDREW LLOYD WEBBER is known as the man behind beloved musicals such as The Phantom of the Opera, Cats and Joseph.

    As well as his illustrious West End career the composer has made headlines for his love life, which has seen him marry three times - and welcome five children.

    Who are Andrew Lloyd Webber's children?

    Andrew Lloyd Webber has five children, who are from from his first and third marriages.

    He did not welcome any children with his second wife.

    Imogen Lloyd Webber

    Imogen, Andrew's oldest child, was born on March 31, 1977.

    She is the daughter of Andrew and his first wife Sarah Hugill.

    Imogen is a broadcaster, author and marketing executive who lives in New York.

    She is a contributor to Broadway publications, a royal correspondent and vice president at entertainment company Concord.

    She often appears on the Fox News channels, and has written several books including The Single Girl's Guide and The Twitter Diaries: 2 Cities, 1 Friendship, 140 Characters.

    Nicholas Lloyd Webber

    After welcoming Imogen, Andrew and Sarah went on to have another child, son Nicholas, born on July 2, 1979.

    Most read in Showbiz

    Nicholas was a Grammy nominated composer and record producer who scored the BBC One drama Love, Lies And Records.

    He also worked on Fat Friends The Musical and scored the Timothy Spall film The Last Bus.

    In June 2018 Nick married viola player Polly Wiltshire.

    Nicholas' father Andrew revealed in March 2023 that his son was seriously ill with stomach cancer.

    Just days later, it was announced that Nicholas had passed away aged 43.

    Sharing the news with his Twitter followers, Andrew wrote: "I am shattered to have to announce that my beloved elder son Nick died a few hours ago in Basingstoke Hospital.

    "His whole family is gathered together and we are all totally bereft. â€“ALW."

    Alastair Adam Lloyd Webber

    Alastair, born on May 3, 1992, is the oldest son from Andrew's third marriage to Madeleine Gurdon.

    He works as a director at The Other Songs Limited, which boasts 75 artists on its roster, with a combined one billion streams worldwide.

    Before this role, Alastair was a manager at Universal-Island Records for almost eight years.

    William Richard Lloyd Webber

    William, born on August 24, 1993, is Andrew and Madeleine's second son.

    He listed as a director at The Other Songs, along with his older brother, Alastair.

    William also appears to have worked for a number of other music labels and brands, including Another Rhythm Limited and The Really Useful Group.

    Isabella Aurora Lloyd Webber

    Andrew's youngest child is daughter Isabella, born on April 30, 1996.

    She is creative director of her own company Bella Webber, which she set up in 2021, and has worked at Sony Music Entertainment and BeHookd Digital in the past.

    Isabella has a graphic design qualification from Shillington College and a politics degree from the University of Bath.

    Who is Andrew Lloyd Webber's wife Madeleine Gurdon?

    Madeleine Gurdon, who married Andrew on February 9, 1991, is an equestrian who has competed in dressage, show-jumping and cross-country riding.

    She and her husband set up a polo club called Watership Down Polo Club and she breeds and trains horses for flat-racing, as well as owning two stud farms.

    Madeleine and Andrew have two sons and a daughter together - Alastair, William and Isabella.

    Who are Andrew Lloyd Webber's ex-wives?

    Sarah Hugill

    Andrew and Sarah Hugill married on July 24, 1971, and had two children together, Imogen and Nicholas.

    The pair divorced on November 14, 1983.

    Sarah Brightman

    Andrew married soprano singer Sarah on March 22, 1984.

    The pair remained friends after their divorce in 1990 and continued to work together in the theatre.

    Sarah started her musical career as part of the group Hot Gossip before embarking on a solo career.
