    What is BIA's net worth?

    Bianca Landrau, known professionally as BIA, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and model.

    Since her rise to fame started on the Oxygen reality television show Sisterhood of Hip Hop, she has an estimated net worth of $1 million.

    Back in 2019, BIA released the single Best On Earth, in collaboration with Russ, and after Rihanna caught wind of it, she posted the song in a video on her Instagram account that gained over 19 million views.

    Currently, that is her highest charted song after it peaked at number 46 on the Billboard Hot 100 list, it was certified platinum by the RIAA after selling more than one million units.

    Following her rise to success, she signed to the label i Am Other, that was founded by Pharrell Williams.

    “They found me off YouTube and they just groomed me,” she told NYLON of working with Pharrell and manager Fam-Lay back in 2019. “I would've never really known who I was if I didn't take that leap and go find myself.”

    How old is BIA?

    Born August 16, 1991, BIA is currently 29-years-old.

    Native of Medford, MA, BIA currently resides in Los Angeles.

    BIA opened up to hip-hop personality DJ Vald back in 2019 about being a mixed race kid growing up in New England.

    “It’s that constant search for identity,” she said.  “I just got a taste of everything and I feel like that made me who I am.”
