    RUGGED rocker PAOLO NUTINI has realised he might have been a bit hasty when he gave up on his latest romance.

    The Scottish star has secretly reconciled with his model ex AMBER ANDERSON after things fizzled out back in March.

    Romantic Paolo reached out to Amber and hit her with the bombshell confession that “life is s**t without you”.

    Or as s**t as the life of a rich and famous musician could possibly be.

    A source said: “There was no animosity when Paolo and Amber split. They just felt they’d reached the end of the road.

    “But after some time apart Paolo realised just how much of a void Amber had left and made it his mission to win her back.

    “She was delighted to hear from him and he didn’t have to try that hard to woo her again.”

    The couple had been keeping their reunion under wraps until recently, when Paolo – who was previously linked to Strictly star LAURA WHITMORE – started popping up on Amber’s Snapchat.

    The pair made their debut at Glastonbury in 2014 after meeting during filming of Paolo’s racy Diana music video. In the clip the pair snogged and nibbled each other in a steamy bath.

    RITA FINDS TEENY HELPER

    TAKING on a figure as mighty as JAY Z must have been a daunting task.

    But RITA ORA had an unlikely shoulder to cry on after suing the Roc Nation boss to get out of her contract last year – ex-teen sensation JOJO.

    The American gave advice and support to Rita, who is now signed with Atlantic, after finding out they both had music held “hostage” by labels.

    JoJo, who is attempting a comeback 12 years after Leave Get Out hit No1, said: “I had to sue my label twice.

    "They weren’t allowing me to release music. I had to essentially fight to have my voice back.

    “Rita and I talked about her lawsuit. We did a show and backstage she was like, ‘I appreciate you took that stance’.

    “I like her a lot. I feel for anybody not able to express themselves.”
