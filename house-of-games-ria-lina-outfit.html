    17 Apr 2024, 00:00

    HOUSE of Games viewers were left seriously distracted by a celebrity's "weird and wonderful" outfit on the show.

    Every week four new famous faces join host Richard Osman to play a host of different games - and one of them was a soon a huge talking point on social media.

    Every day this week the celebrity contestants - Milton Jones, Ria Lina, Martin Roberts and Briony May Williams - take on a series of quiz rounds.

    A daily winner will be declared following a quick-fire round at the end of each show as the scores are tallied across the week resulting in an overall champion being crowned on Friday.

    On Thursday evening's instalment of the BBC quiz show, comedian Ria wore a striking red top and completed the outfit with some eye-catching boots.

    And her choice of footwear soon turned into hot discussion on Twitter, with many viewers applauding the comic for the bold choice.

    Alongside an array of heart emojis, one person penned: "I need to know where Ria Lina gets all her sparkly boots! I love them."

    Echoing their comments, someone else said: "I've been reliably informed by my better half that Ria Lina is again winning in the game of 'Top Boots' on today's House of Games."

    While a third added: "Whatever the results are this week, Ria wins for those fabulous boots."

    The episode in question saw comedian Jen Brister wear a rather bold blue and green jumper.

    The garment featured a collar and some buttons, however, moments after starting the final round - Answer Smash - Jen was suddenly wearing a blue long sleeved shirt.

    Nobody made any reference to the sudden wardrobe switch, but no sooner had viewers clocked the change, Jen was then seen back in the jumper again.
