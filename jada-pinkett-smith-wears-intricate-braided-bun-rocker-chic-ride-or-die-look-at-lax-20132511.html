    Like a rock (chick)! Jada Pinkett Smith walked off the pesky chatter over her megastar husband Will Smith's alleged fling with a carefree attitude at Los Angeles International Airport on Sunday, Nov. 24, sporting a very unique hairstyle and rocker chic look.

    The 42-year-old Hawthorne star made an impressive entrance in thigh-high, black, leather boots with studded chains by Chanel, and a scoop-neck black tank top that showed a peek of her bra as she went through airport security. She topped her daring look with a long, black leather coat and multi-chain gold cross necklace — atop her head, was a nest of braids, elegantly piled and pushed back into a bun, while the sides of her head were still shaved.

    Pinkett Smith buzzed her tresses back in early October, flaunting her beauty versatility at subsequent events including the Make Equality Reality event on Nov. 4 and her stepson Trey's 21st birthday bash in Last Vegas on Nov. 10.

    The look at LAX, however, was not as shocking given Pinkett Smith's rocker roots: Will Smith's wife is the lead singer in a metal band that she personally formed in 2002. Formerly called Wicked Wisdom, the six-member troupe (including Pinkett Smith) has recently transitioned into its new name — Wicked Evolution.
