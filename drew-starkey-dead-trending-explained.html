    DREW Starkey is one of America’s brightest actors bursting onto TV screens with a starring role in hit Netflix series Outer Banks.

    But ahead of his next big acting role in romantic comedy The Other Zoey the star has been wrongly trending online for being killed in a freak accident.

    Why is ‘Drew Starkey’s dead’ trending?

    A fake news report was published on October 19, 2023, saying the actor was involved in a fatal car accident.

    This led fans to mourn the young celeb's untimely death even though he is very much alive and well.

    The article bluntly told readers: “The recent car accident that Drew Starkey was involved in has left the entertainment industry and his fans in a state of uncertainty.”

    Despite it having no evidence to back this up or any statement from police, friends or family, thousands still believed the hoax.

    Has 'Drew Starkey’s dead' trended before?

    This is the second time the star has been a victim to a shocking rumor over his fake death.

    The twisted rumors first started after a TikTok was posted in December 2022, which led fans to believe the actor had passed away.

    The clip, which showed a series of images of Starkey, had the caption: “That last video is one of my favorites ever of him…genuinely so sad I’ll never meet/know him.”

    The video quickly racked up a huge number of views in a matter of hours as fans misread the caption and assumed that Drew was dead.

    The TikTok was actually just referring to the user never getting to see him in general not because he was no longer alive.

    He clarified: “Wait why is everyone thinking he’s dead?! I’m just saying I’ll probably never meet him??”

    Regardless word spread like wildfire to other social media’s where his “death” began to trend.

    Who is Drew Starkey?

    Drew Starkey is an American actor from Asheville, North Carolina.  

    The 30-year-old was born in Hickory and attended college at Western Carolina University, where he studied theatre and English.

    His most famous role to date is playing Rafe Cameron in the teen drama series Outer Banks on Netflix.

    He’s also starred in romantic comedy Love, Simon and been in Netflix's Ozark and VH1 TV series Scream: Resurrection.

    Starkey has taken the social media world by storm since becoming a big time celebrity, with fans posting edits of him in various acting roles and interviews online followed by captions showing appreciation for the man’s skills and often his good looks.

    The star has 3.8million followers on Instagram but isn’t very active on his account and since December 2022 has only five posts and one reel uploaded.
