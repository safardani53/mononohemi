    STACEY Solomon has revealed that she only washes her hair ONCE a week - but feels "so much better" when she finally cleans it.

    The 31-year-old star, who is currently pregnant with her fourth child, documented hair wash day on her Instagram Story after finding herself with more time than usual now her sons have broken up from school for the summer.

    She told followers: "I had no school run today so I actually washed, dried, and straightened my hair and I feel like a new woman.

    "I have absolutely nowhere to go but I'm swooshing myself around the house like Mariah Carey!"

    Stacey then shared a video montage of the wash, joking: "It was such a big occasion of course I filmed it."

    As she straightened her locks, she added: "Luckily because it's so thick I only have to do this once and I can leave it for a good week now.
