    HOMELANDER well and truly met his match in the latest episode of The Boys.

    The dastardly leader of The Seven left fans aghast last week when he murdered the shape-shifter he had been romping with in the wake of Madelyn Stillwell's death.

    However, Homelander (Antony Starr) didn't waste any time moving on with his new teammate Stormfront (Aya Cash).

    The pair got off to a bad start when Stormfront disobeyed Homelander's orders and killed Kimiko's brother, before publicly taking credit and stealing his thunder.

    However, Stormfront later offered to help get Homelander back on track with his fanbase, offering the services of her social media team and proposing and alliance.

    The partnership took quite the sordid turn though as Stormfront urged him to blast her chest with his laser vision.

    Before long the pair were romping like only superheroes can - throwing each other around the room and even doing the deed mid-air.

    Starr admitted the logistics of the scene were tricky to pull off and required protective equipment to ensure the pair didn't injure each other.

    "That’s probably one of the more interesting romantic things I’ve ever done," he told TV Line.

    "Let me put it this way: Whenever I’ve done a love scene in the past, I’ve never had to talk to the stunts team and get body armor put on and elbow and knee pads. I can say that was a whopping great first."
