    A FASHIONISTA has fallen in love with Skims and prefers it to her Victoria's Secret garments.

    The trendsetter wears a 40D, and she said her new bra is much softer and less expensive than her closet staple.

    TikToker Nahiely Alexandra (@nahiely.alexandra) was not disappointed when she "caved" and purchased two items from Skims.

    "These Skims bras are top tier and still the bras I reach for the most," she captioned the clip.

    "Kimberly Kardashian: What in the world did you put on these bras?" Nahiely asked at the beginning of the video.

    The fashionista tried on the Fits Everybody T-shirt push-up bra and the Fits Everybody Unlined bra, both in a side 40D.

    Nahiely gave the unlined bra a "10 out of 10."

    "I don't typically buy bras like this because it's very exposing, but I actually really love how this feels on me right now," she said.

    "If I'm being honest, this bra makes me feel really sexy," she added.

    Nahiely also liked the bra with the underwire and gave it a "10 out of 10" as well.

    Most read in Fashion

    "I'm comparing this to my Victoria's Secret push-up bra," she said.

    "It's actually cheaper and it feels softer and it feels so supported."

    The influencer finished her video by telling people to "run don't walk" to get the bras from Skims.

    Skims is the shapewear, loungewear, and underwear company created by Kim Kardashian.

    The bras range in cup size from A to H, and the band size goes from 30 to 46.

    The unlined T-shirt bra comes in various colors and costs $54.

    The push-up bra from the Fits Everybody collection comes in nudes, as well as colorful shades, and costs $56.
