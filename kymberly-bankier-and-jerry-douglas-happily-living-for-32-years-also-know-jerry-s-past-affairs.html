    Age difference can never be a hurdle for people to fall in love. We have seen a lot of celebrities and stars being together despite the huge age gap. Yet, some survive, some don’t. However, today we are here to tell you a happy story of two lovers who have been going strong for the past 32 years. The power couple is no other than Kymberly Bankier and her husband, Jerry Douglas.

    Kym and Jerry’s relationship stands out as an inspiration for a lot of couples. They have one child together, who is not a child anymore. The couple’s son is already a grown-up. Let’s dig deeper into their story:

    Kymberly Bankier and Jerry Douglas marriage

    Kym and Jerry’s love story is quite ordinary yet cute. They met for the first at a Muscular Dystrophy Association event. They fell in love quickly after their first meeting. It didn’t take them too long, we must accept!

    They tied the knot on in a low-key wedding ceremony. Although Kym is twenty-six years younger than her husband, she didn’t see it as a barrier to their love affair.

    Kymberly Bankier and Jerry Douglas children

    Kym got the privilege to become a mother. The beautiful baby was a son, and they named him Hunter Douglas.

    Jerry Douglas relationship with his first wife, Arlene

    If you think Hunter is the only son of Kym, then you need an update.  Kym is actually a stepmother of Jerry’s two children, Avra and Jordan, from his former marriage. Yes, Jerry was married to Arlene. Jerry was quite serious with his first wife because he even quit acting to save his marriage with her. However, that didn’t help, and the pair ended up in a divorce.

    As we know everything happens for a reason, Jerry’s devastating first marriage made him meet Kym. Now, the couple is happily married for 32 years. How amazing is that? We don’t think Jerry Douglas’ second marriage with Kymberly Bankier is going to end in the same way because the love between them seems to be unbreakable.
