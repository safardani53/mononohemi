    KANYE West's wife Bianca Censori has been praised for her maternal behavior towards her stepdaughter North.

    The Yeezy designer, 28, held hands with the nine-year-old as they headed to Kanye's 46th birthday bash on Saturday night.

    Bianca walked slightly in front of North as they chatted and smiled while walking to the party.

    North wore a baggy gray sweatshirt, black ripped shorts, and black boots.

    The preteen showed off her natural curls in a ponytail and appeared to be makeup free apart from blood on her face thanks to special effects products.

    Fans praised Bianca's nurturing and protective behavior towards North in public.

    YE'S BIRTHDAY

    Kanye celebrated his birthday in a low-key fashion, with “candlelight, music, and epic food," a source revealed to The U.S. Sun.
