    Take a look back at Mark Wahlberg’s transformation over the years in these photos!

    Mark Wahlberg is an actor, who is the youngest of nine children. However, he began his career as a musician, going by the stage name Marky Mark in the early 90s. After transitioning into a career as an actor, Mark began getting major recognition for his work. He’s received nominations at the Academy Awards, Golden Globes, Emmys and SAG Awards. Some of his notable roles include ‘The Departed,’ ‘The Perfect Storm’ and ‘Transformers.’

    In addition to his career, Mark is also a proud father. He married Rhea Durham in 2009 and they have four children together. Mark is never afraid to transform for a role, whether that means gaining weight, losing weight or packing on muscle. This dedication has led to Mark sporting many different looks over the years, undergoing quite a transformation from time to time.
