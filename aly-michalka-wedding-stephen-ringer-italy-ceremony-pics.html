    Nearly one year later after Aly became engaged to Stephen in June 2014, the couple tied the knot in front of several friends and family. Instead of wearing white on her big day like most brides do, Aly instead stunned in a beautiful lavender gown. She matched the colorful embellished gown with a pretty beaded headband and a purple flower bouquet. Gorgeous!

    The outdoor Italian ceremony looked so romantic. Aly and Stephen’s close friends and family posted pics of the couple on their big day, sharing extravagant pics along with #PutARingOnIt. Get it? Cause Aly is a Ringer now? The adorable pics (seen in our gallery) show Aly walking down the aisle, ready to say her “I do’s,” as well as cutting the cake with Stephen later on in the ceremony!

    Aly’s maid of honor was her sister and bandmate, AJ Michalka, who threw her an amazing bachelorette party in May with a few of her close gal pals. Aly was also surrounded by her BFF, who shared a pic of Aly walking down the aisle, saying “Congratulations to one of my dearest friends and the most beautiful bride. Today was a fairytale.” Yes, indeed it was!

    Aly got her big break in television starring on Disney’s Phil of the Future. She later went on to land roles in Two and a Half Men and iZombie. She is also a member of rock band 78violet with AJ.

    HollywoodLifers, share your congrats to Aly and Stephen in the comments below!
