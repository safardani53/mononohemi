    The Ultra Mini Uggs are a hybrid between the Ugg Tasman and the Ugg Classic Boot and have been a huge sell-out success for many months.

    The Ultra Mini Ugg Boot features a lower shaft measuring 3.5 inches in height, reaching just above the ankle yet not quite touching the calf.

    It has the classic closed-toe style to keep your tootsies warm and dry from the elements while also keeping your ankle protected, and they won't slide off your feet.

    The interior boasts the super-soft and insulating signature sheepskin and ultra-soft Uggplush lining, while the sole is lightweight with a slight platform.

    The outsole has been created using Ugg's iconic Treadlite, which delivers optimum comfort.

    This style comes in numerous colours to co-ordinate with every outfit.

    You can pair the Ultra Mini Uggs with everything from jeans and a jumper, to leggings and a T-shirt for those casual errand running days, or even a tracksuit for lazy Sundays.

    We'd even go as far as to say you can pair the Ultra Mini Ugg with more than other Ugg designs because the low rise doesn't interfere with outfits and so can still be worn with wide-leg jeans, long-length trousers and more.

    The question on every shopper's lips is where to buy Uggs – in particular where to buy the Ultra Mini Uggs – and will there be a restock – and we have all the details.

    Where to buy Ultra Mini Uggs online?

    Ultra Mini Uggs have been selling out due to popular demand.

    However, we've spotted them back on the virtual shelves of several online retailers – including the Ugg website itself.

    We recommend trying the Ugg website first as this is the first place the design will be restocked, and it offers all colours and sizes as well as other styles such as the platform version and children's variation.

    At the time of writing the Ultra Mini Uggs are in stock in every colour on the Ugg website, although sizes are limited.

    Other popular places to buy Uggs from include Schuh, ASOS and Office, plus plenty more retailers.

    How to style Ultra Mini Uggs

    Like most Ugg boots, the Ultra Minis are super versatile and can be styled in a number of ways to suit your needs and the occasion.

    Admittedly we wouldn't wear Ugg's Ultra Mini Boots on a night "out out", but we'd certainly pack them in a very oversized bag to wear on our journey home.

    The sell-out Ultra Mini Ugg Boots can be worn on those casual days lazing around or when errands have to be done.

    You can style them with leggings, a crop top and a shirt, or even with your workout attire, as well as a tracksuit set.

    You can also pair them with jeans, either skinny or wide-leg, a crisp white T-shirt and a blazer for a weekend shopping trip or brunch with the girls.

    Where are Ultra Mini Uggs in stock?

    Ultra Mini Uggs have been selling like hotcakes over the last few months.

    The usual retailers we head to shop Ultra Mini Uggs include Ugg (of course) as well as Office, Schuh, Flannels and ASOS.

    At the time of writing, the Ultra Mini Uggs are in stock at Ugg, although sizes are limited.

    Selfridges has a few sizes of the classic colours tan and black available to shop, as well as platform designs and children's versions, so you will want to shop quickly so not to miss out.

    Schuh has the black and chestnut colours still available, although in very limited sizes.
