    A TIKTOK creator has claimed they found a dress dupe that looks designer - but is a fraction of the price.

    The creator claims they found a dupe of a dress by designer House of CB for under $100.

    House of CB designs evening wear and high-end dresses that retail for hundreds of dollars - the designer's website lists dresses over $200.

    One of the newest dresses features a corseted bodice, a flowery print, and a knee-length skirt.

    The dress retails for $225.

    A TikTok creator, Fashion Finds (@fashionfinderqueen), claims they found a dupe for just $75.

    "Finding dupes so you don't have to," the content creator claimed in the video's on-screen text.

    "I’ll do a try on video later this week, but it pretty much looks exactly like this!"

    The creator claims a dress from Allure Beverly Hills looks very similar - including the corset lace on the back.

    TikTokers rushed to the comments section to commend the dress for its similarities.
