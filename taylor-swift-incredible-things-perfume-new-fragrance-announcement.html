    ‘Taylor Swift Incredible Things’ — Singer Launches New Perfume

    The new bottle is modern mature and modern than her previous three scents — we love it! The bottle pays homage to Taylor’s love of watercolor painting. The gold cap has an embossed number 13, Taylor’s favorite number.

    The notes are as follows:

    “Top: vanilla orchid, suede flower and creamy musk

    Middle: pink pepper, sparkling grapefruit and wild passion flower

    Base: white amber, Madagascar vanilla and Haitian vetiver.”

    According to a press release from the brand:

    “Taylor lives her life with an open heart, and by sharing her personal stories through music, art and fragrance, she helps inspire her fans to follow their heart, to be their own artist — to do incredible things. Channeling Taylor’s inherent ability to see beauty in the world, she encourages her fans to do the same.”

    Taylor Swift’s New Fragrance — Where To Buy

    You can get Taylor’s new scent at Kohl’s starting in October! It comes in two sizes of Eau de Parfum Sprays (1.0 fl. oz and 1.7 fl. oz) and a travel-friendly rollerball.
