    GOOD Morning Britain viewers were left doing a double-take today - when a naked guest appeared in the studio.

    Helen Berriman appeared on the show to debate whether nude sunbathing should be illegal.

    However, she was even naked on the show, with producers carefully blurring parts of her body when she was in the shot.

    One GMB fan asked: "Why is there a naked blurry women on ITV right now? Bit dramatic."

    Another shocked viewer wrote: "As if she's fully naked."

    Helen is a member of the Naturist Foundation - whose motto is "bare is beautiful" - and was debating naked sunbathing with brand and culture expert Nick Ede.

    GMB presenter Ed Balls opened the debate saying: "We're going to strip things right back now. Would you call the police on someone sunbathing naked in a public space?

    "That's what happened when a nudity row broke out at Hampstead Heath pond in London as complaints about a sunbather who refused to cover up forced staff to call 999."

    Helen reasoned: "With all the things that are going on in the world at the moment are we really that bothered about a nude body sunbathing in a quiet corner of a park?

    "There is so much else that we could be concerned about."
