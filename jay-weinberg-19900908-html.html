    Jay Weinberg is an American musician and drummer of the heavy metal Slipknot band. He is the son of American drummer Max Weinberg. He performed with the American punk rock band The Reveling and toured in 2009 as a drummer with Bruce Springsteen's E Street Band, replacing his father. In 2010, he briefly served as a drummer for Madball. In 2011 and 2012, Weinberg played against Against Me !. In 2014, Weinberg replaced Joey Jordison as Slipknot's drummer.
