    CHRISTINE Brown has shown off her dancing skills in a recent racy sneak peek clip.

    In a two-part Sister Wives special, fans will get a glimpse of Christine, 51, and her husband, David Woolley's, 59, wedding and all the preparation that lead up to their special day.

    Back in November, TLC revealed that part one will air on January 7 and part two on January 14.

    On Sunday, after the season finale of the dramatic reality TV series, fans were given a sneak peek at what to expect during the wedding special.

    In it, Christine discussed where she met David, her family's emotional responses, her fellow sister wives reactions - including Janelle, 54, Meri, 52, Robyn, 42, and ex-Kody, 54, - and even Christine and David on the dance floor.

    One scene showed the TV personality getting up close and personal with her hubby as he sat on a chair and allowed his wife to give him a lap dance in her stunning wedding dress.

    The two lovebirds tied the knot on October 7 in a beautiful ceremony in Red Cliffs Lodge, a magical hotel in Moab, Utah just six months after getting engaged.

    UNDER WRAPS

    The US Sun got an exclusive look into their wedding as 330 guests were invited - including Janelle and her children she shares with Kody - Aspyn, 28; Mykelti, 27; Paedon, 25; Gwendlyn, 21; Ysabel, 20; and Truely, 13.

    In attempts to keep the wedding under wraps, security was hired for the special day to not let any intruders crash or capture pictures.

    An insider told The U.S. Sun exclusively: "There were security officers on the property making sure everything ran smoothly and there were no trespassers.

    "The entrance in front of the lodge was not accessible, so there was no way of even getting close, unless you were a guest of the hotel."

    The source added: "They wanted to keep the wedding a big secret from the general public so that nothing got leaked.

    "There was no way of even getting a glimpse of Christine's wedding because anywhere you would be able to view it, it appeared to be closed to the public at the time."

    WEDDING OF A LIFETIME

    An insider told The U.S. Sun that the outdoor wedding at the whimsical Red Cliffs Lodge was an all-day affair with setup beginning in the early hours of the morning.

    "Everything was placed strategically and, of course, to get the best views for cameras," the insider said.

    After the wedding took place in the early afternoon, the guests were tasked with cheering for cameras.

    "The guests were told to make cheering noises multiple times," the insider told The U.S. Sun.

    "It seemed like some parts of the wedding were definitely staged for TV, such as getting reaction from the crowd."

    The long production day ended shortly after sunset.

    Meri, Robyn, and Kody were not in attendance.

    Christine was the first to leave the polygamist family in 2021 - followed by Janelle and Meri.

    'A FAIRYTALE'

    When the couple got engaged, Christine told People: "It's a fairytale. It's everything I never thought I would have. I get to walk down the aisle to David, him just looking at me the way that he does, it’s a dream come true."
