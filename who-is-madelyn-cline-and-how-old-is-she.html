    Who is Madelyn Cline and how old is she?

    Madelyn Renee Cline was born December 21, 1997, in Goose Creek, South Carolina.

    The 24-year-old is known for her starring role as Sarah Cameron on Outer Banks, for which she was cast in 2018.

    The first season was released on April 15, 2020, which garnered a lot of positive attention and reviews.

    Outer Banks was renewed for a second season in July 2020 and premiered on July 30, 2021.

    Early in her career, Cline worked on television commercials for T-Mobile and Sunny D.

    She dropped out of Coastal Carolina University in 2017 to pursue acting.

    Her earliest roles were small in Boy Erased, Vice Principals, The Originals, and Stranger Things.

    During an interview with Women’s Health, Cline announced she had an eating disorder in her teens, which led to mental health issues.

    Who is Madelyn Cline’s ex-boyfriend?

    Cline used to date Chase Strokes, who plays John B, in the Netflix series Outer Banks.

    In September 2020, Cline and Stokes were in the music video for Kygo and Donna Summer’s Hot Stuff hit single.

    The two revealed their relationship on Instagram.

    Stoke’s posted a picture in June 2020 of the two enjoying a picnic together.

    He wrote, “cats outta the bag” with a red heart emoji.

    Cline posted a picture of the two and wrote, “The one.”

    In an interview with Access Hollywood, Cline said when she first met Stokes, "He was in a really bad mood. Kinda bratty,” leaving her “confused with” him.

    In May 2021, the couple won an MTV Movie & TV Award for the Best Kiss, which they celebrated on stage. 

    The two decided to end their relationship after season 2 of Outer Banks in late 2021.

    After their break-up, Cline was linked to DJ Zack Bia, although the two did not officially confirm the relationship.

    The two were spotted together at Cline's 24th birthday party, as reported by E! News.

    Bia is now linked to pop icon Olivia Rodrigo.

    What is Outer Banks about?

    The Netflix series is an action-adventure mystery teen drama, which first streamed on April 15, 2020.

    The show takes place along the Outer Banks of North Carolina where there is a strong divide between wealthy residents and working-class locals.

    A group of Pogue teens is determined to find out what happened to John B’s father.
