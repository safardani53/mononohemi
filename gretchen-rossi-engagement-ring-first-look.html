    As previously reported, Gretchen Rossi is engaged to Slade Smiley.

    Not so previously reported, yet proved in the photo below? Her ring is huge!

    The Real Housewives of Orange County star was actually the one to propose to her long-time boyfriend two weeks ago, popping the major question atop the AT&T Building in Los Angeles.

     

    “I’ve never been so overwhelmed with just sheer joy and happiness!” Smiley tells Life & Style of the moment.

    Five days after the couple agreed to marry, though, Slade shocked his bride-to-be with a trip to Newport Jewelers in Newport Beach.

    Once there, he presented Rossi with a four-carat yellow diamond ring. Not shabby, dude!

    Any shame, Gretchen, over asking for Smiley’s hand and heart in marriage?

    “Life is too short, I’m in love with this man, and I needed to take the plunge!” she told the magazine, whose latest issue is on sale now.
