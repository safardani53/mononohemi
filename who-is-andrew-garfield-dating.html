    THE Amazing Spider-Man actor Andrew Garfield has secured quite a few girlfriends over the years.

    His career and love life have continued to blossom.

    Who is Andrew Garfield dating?

    Alyssa Miller

    As of December 2021, former Spider-Man actor Andrew Garfield is dating Alyssa Miller.

    The couple were first spotted together in November 2021 when they were photographed holding hands in New York City. 

    Alyssa is a model, appearing for several brands including Guess, La Perla, Elie Tahari, Laura Biagiotti, and Chopard.

    She has also posed in magazines such as Vogue, Sports Illustrated’s Swimsuit Issue in 2011.

    The model launched her travel line, Pilgrim, including handbags, luggage, and travel accessories in 2018.

    Miller was previously married to musician Cam Avery in April 2018 before splitting in November of that same year.

    She’s also been romantically linked to Jake Gyllenhaal, who ironically played Mysterio in 2019’s Spider-Man: No Way Home.

    The pair split in 2014.

    Who has Andrew Garfield dated?

    Shannon Woodward

    Shannon Woodward is an actress who has starred on shows like Westworld and Raising Hope.

    The pair started dating in 2008 and split in 2011. 

    Emma Stone

    After splitting with Shannon, he shortly met his next girlfriend to be - Emma Stone.

    Andrew and Emma started their relationship in 2011 while filming The Amazing Spider-Man.

    Much like Zendaya and Tom Holland, the couple developed an attraction to each other on set as they played love interests Peter Parker and Gwen Stacy.

    Garfield and Stone split in 2015 after four years together. 

    Rita Ora

    Andrew and singer Rita Ora were first linked in November 2018 after being spotted together in London.

    They broke up in March 2019 after only four months together. 

    Rita is now dating filmmaker Taika Waititi and seems to be happy in love.
