    A MID-size fashionista has gone on a fall shopping spree to refresh her wardrobe for the new season.

    One of her top picks was a dupe of an item from Free People, which she said was "so pretty."

    TikToker Brittany (@brittanyjohnson_xoxo) shares fashion advice and vlogs with her 422,000 followers.

    The influencer shared that she is 5-foot-3 and 175 pounds, midsize at a size 12.

    She loved to share midsize fashion tips and Amazon fashion with her followers.

    In her recent video, she did a fall fashion try-on haul after sourcing clothes from Amazon.

    The clip opened with Brittany clutching an armful of clothing.

    She said: "I just got these in from Amazon! Let's try them on and I'll let you know what I think."

    The first item the influencer tried was a sage green top which she said was "really soft."

    The long-sleeved shirt had gathered smocked cuffs and a loose fit.

    Available on Amazon for $29.99, the shirt came in multiple colorways.

    She was "obsessed" with the top and how it could be dressed up or down.

    Next, Brittany pulled out a black cardigan.

    She said: "Run to my storefront, add this to your cart - you need it like yesterday."

    The layering item came in black and cream versions, and the content creator said it was a dupe for a cardigan from Free People.

    Brittany said: "It's so cute and I love the texture detail. I'm going to wear this so much for fall."

    The cardigan was on Amazon for $33.98 in the black color Brittany chose.

    The fashionista continued with her haul, trying on a waffle knit loose turtleneck top in a muted coral color.

    She said: "This shirt is adorable and it's such a pretty color."
