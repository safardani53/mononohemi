    Netflix’s forthcoming Spanish romantic picture Love at First Embrace, one of the most anticipated titles this month, is set to debut on the platform this Friday, March 3, at 3 a.m. ET. The storyline of Love at First Embrace, directed by Alauda Ruiz de Aza and penned by Cristóbal Garrido and Adolfo Valor, tells the tale of a guy searching for love and finding it in the most improbable of locations.

    The love comedy is directed by Antonio Asensio and Paloma Molina under the banner of Zeta Productions, the music is written by Ivan Palomares, and the photographer is Sergi Gallardo. Furthermore, the 96-minute film stars lvaro Cervantes as Javier in the main part.

    The lead in Netflix’s Love at First Embrace film has the ability to see into the future.

    Javier (Cerbantes), a one-of-a-kind and talented protagonist, has the ability to see into the future, but only after kissing a lady. He can predict the result of any love engagement with just one smooch. But authority comes at a cost, making it difficult for him to maintain a relationship, spurring him to terminate it before a potential break-up to prevent heartache. Javier’s finding about himself when he was only 16 years old becomes a problem with time, making it nearly impossible for him to find love and eventually settle down with a significant other.

    When he caresses Luca (Silvia Alonso), he envisions a future with her in which they are blissfully married and have children. But there’s one problem: Luca is Roberto’s boyhood best friend’s fiancée.

    According to Wikipedia, the formal description of the forthcoming romance film is as follows:

    “Watch the tale of a 16-year-old who discovers that when he caresses a female for the first time, he can see the entire future of a love relationship.”

    The original teaser for Love at First Embrace begins with an adolescent Javier discovering his special talent.

    The video for Love at First Embrace begins with Javier, sixteen, playing the game Truth or Dare with his pals, where he first caresses a female and finds his strength. He has used his ability to foresee the future and shield himself from possible sorrow and pain ever since. However, as a result of the talent that leads him to abandon a companion even in the case of the smallest problem, he has grown distant and uneasy with everyone around him over the years.

    Although Javier uses his talent to avoid heartache, completely relying on it to direct him to the right route and the right life partner, he has a tragic run-in with his boyhood buddy and his fiancée Lucia. In the forthcoming picture, lvaro Cervantes portrays Javier, and Silvia Alonso plays Lucia.
