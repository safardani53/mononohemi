    When is Black Friday 2021?

    Black Friday falls on November 26, 2021.

    The big shopping event of the season is always the day after Thanksgiving, aka Turkey Day, celebrated by Americans.
    Thanksgiving is on the fourth Thursday in November, and Black Friday follows.

    For those looking to save on a new vacuum or garden appliance; start looking up Black Friday deals ahead of the big day - many retailers launch sales up to two weeks before the event.
