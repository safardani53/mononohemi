    Brooke Burns visited the set of Good Day L.A. on Wednesday to share more  than details about her new game show You Deserve It: her gorgeous engagement ring!

    The former Baywatch star, who was previously married to Australian actor Julian McMahon from 1999 to 2001 and engaged to Bruce Willis in 2004, revealed on the show that she is ready to tie the knot with writer and director Gavin O'Connor.

    "I was trying to keep it on the down low," she said. "He is a writer and director. He has a little girl who is a year and a half younger than [my daughter] Maddy."

    But Burns, 33, was happy to show off her 3-carat heart-shaped diamond, and jewelry expert Michael O'Connor has all the details.
