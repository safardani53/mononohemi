    A DOLLAR Tree super fan has revealed the top beauty items shoppers need in their lives as well as the ones they should potentially avoid in their shopping cart.

    Dollar Tree is a go-to spot for deals, with the majority of items being priced at just $1.25.

    Still, if you’re looking to upgrade your beauty supplies, there are some products you should specifically look out for.

    TikToker @PeachyyMaddi regularly creates videos on the best Dollar Tree items for her Boujee on a Budget series.

    In a recent video, she had all the secrets to transform your beauty routine with some essential Dollar Tree items.

    WHAT TO GRAB

    Maddi had one brand that she says is absolutely a must if you’re looking for beauty products at the discount retailer.

    Read more about Dollar Tree

    PS Clean Beauty offers various types of products, and all are vegan and high-quality, according to the content creator.

    “I love this brand,” Maddi said.

    Maddi noted that PS Clean Beauty has options for volumizing, gentle, and ultra-hydrating shampoo, and all varieties are priced at $1.25.

    The brand also sells various sets of conditioners.

    Most read in Money

    “I’ve done a review of these products, and I totally recommend them,” Maddi said.

    While you’re perusing Dollar Tree’s aisles for the PS Clean Beauty brand, you can also stock up on some “really cute” loofahs, Maddi said.

    While one was pink and strawberry-themed, there are plenty of other designs to consider as well.

    Another brand you should have on your list?

    B-Pure, according to Maddi.

    “They have anti-frizz serum as well as curl serum,” she shared with her more than 480,000 followers.

    Shoppers can also consider the tea tree oil scalp serum or scrub.

    NEW PRODUCTS

    There were some products that Maddi said she was unsure about.

    This included the Skin Nutrition Botanicals Tea Tree Oil + Salicylic Acid Balancing Face Wash.

    Maddi hadn’t yet tried it and was curious what other Dollar Tree enthusiasts thought of the item.

    Meanwhile, B-Pure also has calming lavender vulva cream, which Maddi was not confident about.

    However, the lavender feminine bath bomb “could be really good when you’re on your period,” the influencer said.

    If you’re looking for more cramp relief, the B-Pure Menstrual Heat Patch is also a safe bet, and you can score the item for just $1.25.

    “These are a lifesaver,” Maddi said.

    Outside the beauty department, Maddi was drawn in by some children’s toys for the same low price, but she wasn’t sure exactly how she felt about them.

    A sliceable toy food set and kid’s broom are still available for the same low price.

    “I could see some kids being like ‘Yay, I’m going to clean,’ but I can also see it being a total fail,” Maddi said. 

    DOLLAR TREE IN THE NEWS

    While Dollar Tree still offers some of the best deals around, the discount retailer has been harshly critisized by shoppers after increasing its prices.

    Historically, the Dollar Tree provided all products at just $1, but that all changed in late 2021.

    And moving forward, it’s likely customers will see even more price hikes.

    New frozen foods, bread, and other merchandise could reach costs as high as $3, $4, or even $5.

    The company says these price hikes allow Dollar Tree to provide a wider selection of goods and customers to have a full shopping experience.

    “From $2.01 to $5 [price points], there’s another huge market that is untapped for us,” the store’s chief merchandising officer Rick McNeely said.

    By remaining at $1.25 prices, the company was limited to “single serve or a novelty offering,” McNeely said.

    EVEN MORE SAVINGS AT DOLLAR TREE

    While Dollar Tree arguably has some of the best deals available, with products generally selling for just $1.25, there are still ways you can earn more from the retailer.

    For one, you’ll score the best finds if you know the ideal time to shop.

    Dollar Tree typically receives shipments on Tuesday or Wednesday, so you’ll want to do your shopping trip the day after to locate the best products.

    Additionally, timing your trip between 10am and 3pm may be the best option, because this is when Dollar Tree is the least busy.

    To earn the most savings, however, make sure you pay attention to the unit price.

    While Dollar Tree offers products for $1.25, other stores may have them for cheaper, especially if they offer items in bulk sizes.

    To know for sure, divide the total price of each product by the number of the item you’ll receive in each package.
