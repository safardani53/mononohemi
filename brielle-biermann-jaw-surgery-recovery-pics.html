    BRIELLE Biermann has revealed she is getting back to normality after the star underwent double jaw surgery.

    The 24-year-old openly documented the journey of her recovery on her social media platforms and now she says she is "finally getting back to work" after a grueling few weeks of healing.

    Taking to her official Instagram account, she shared the update on her Story for her 1.3million followers to see.

    She shared the update written over a video of her in the back of a car smiling while wearing make-up with her hair down.

    Grinning for the camera to show off her healed surgery, Brielle wrote over the clip: "Splint it out," followed by an emoji sticking its tongue out.

    "Finally getting back to work," she finished off the post with a white heart.

    The star zoomed in on and around her jaw to show it off without the splint.

    Brielle previously revealed she had the surgery to correct her overbite as it made eating difficult for her.
