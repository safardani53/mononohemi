    MANY Made In Chelsea alumni have gone on to carve out successful careers for themselves, including Sophie Habboo.

    She found fame on the reality show as well as finding love with Jamie Laing, but who is she?

    Who is Sophie Habboo?

    Sophie Habboo joined Made in Chelsea during the 14th series.

    The star was born on 19th October and brought up in Leamington Spa in Warwickshire.

    Sophie is a social media influencer and you can follow her on Instagram @habboosophie

    Habbs graduated from Newcastle University in 2015 with a degree in Media, Communications and Cultural Studies.

    She joined the show as a full-cast member alongside friend and fellow Newcastle graduate Clementine Cuthbertson.

    How long has Sophie been dating Jamie Laing?

    News of Sophie's romance with Jamie Laing stunned fans when their romantic feelings were made known in series 17 of Made In Chelsea.

    Despite people's thoughts and opinions Sophie and Jamie stayed strong and tied the knot on Friday, April 14, 2023.

    They also have their own podcast together called Nearly Weds.

    More Made in Chelsea

    Who else has she dated?

    Fellow Made In Chelsea star Alex Mytton and Habbs were firmly loved up in the last episode of series 15 despite the efforts of an intruding Harry Baron.

    However, Alex called time on the relationship and told The Sun: “We are kind of just friends...it fizzled out.”

    Alex claimed he was only moving slowly because he was fresh out of his relationship with Lottie Moss.

    Habbs also had a well-documented relationship with MIC and Celebs Go Dating star Sam Thompson.

    Who are Sophie Habboo's parents?

    Sophie's mother has worked for a nutrition and skincare brand and has also worked as a director of a property management company.

    She also once appeared on an episode of Made In Chelsea.

    Sophie's dad is a founder of a management company.

    Sophie also has a younger sister called Georgia who works as a make-up artist.
