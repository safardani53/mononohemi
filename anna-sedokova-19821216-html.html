    Anna Vladimirovna Sedokova is a Ukrainian singer, actress and television presenter. Sedokova rose to fame in 2002 as a member of the pop girl group Nu Virgos, known in CIS countries as VIA Gra and she is nicknamed Anya. After two years in the group's "golden lineup", she pursued a solo music career in 2006. The female singer released a series of singles until her debut album, Lichnoe, was released on the year 2016 with commercial success, reaching second place in Russia. . Along with her musical career, Sedokova has gained widespread recognition as an actress and TV presenter in Ukrainian and Russian productions. She currently lives in Los Angeles with her three children.
