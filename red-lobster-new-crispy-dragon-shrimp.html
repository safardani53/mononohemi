    SEAFOOD lovers have a new option to choose from in the popular Ultimate Endless Shrimp lineup.

    Red Lobster is changing up its classic offering with a crispy new flavor.

    The Crispy Dragon Shrimp are savory and sweet with a bit of mildly spicy chili sauce.

    Also part of the deal is the garlic shrimp scampi and Walt's Favorite shrimp.

    Each order comes with a choice of two sides and the iconic Cheddar Bay Biscuits.

    Diners can order from the Ultimate Endless Shrimp all day, every day for just $20.

    On X, formerly known as Twitter, fans were excited to hear the news.

    One wrote; "Yum! How about a little toothpick sample for the doordash Dashers?"

    Red Lobster also introduced three new drink options to wash everything down.

    The fruity margaritas are made with Casamigos and are available in; Wintermelon Mint, Spicy Pineapple, and Casa Colada.

    Krispy Kreme has already released its fall menu with two new donuts; pumpkin spice cheesecake swirl, and pumpkin spice maple pecan.
