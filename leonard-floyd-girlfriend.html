    A Short Bio On Leonard Floyd

    NFL athlete Leonard Floyd was born on September 8, 1992, to his parents, Leonard Lloyd and Chrishonda Floyd, in Eastman, Georgia, United States.

    Floyd attended Dodge County High School in Eastman, where he played football alongside track racing.

    While in school, Leonard played equally well in defensive and offensive positions but later became a defenseman.

    After graduating in 2011, he enrolled in the University of Georgia for higher studies. However, he attended the Hargrave Military Academy to meet the NCAA requirements before he began his college career with the Bulldogs.

    After an astounding junior season performance with 72 combined tackles, he left college and enlisted for the NFL draft.

    To his delight, the Chicago Bears chose him as the ninth overall pick even though his later time in the NFL combine was spoilt because of injuries. On May 27, 2016, he signed a four-year contract with the Bears.

    After appearing in four seasons for the Bears, he signed with the Los Angeles Rams on April 24, 2020.
