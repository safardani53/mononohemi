    Nick Bawden's wife, Alexis Bawden, took to TikTok to accuse the famous MTV star of sliding into her husband's Instagram messages.

    The NFL star's wife, who has over 30,000 followers on Instagram, has shared a screenshot allegedly showing a direct message that the Jersey Shore star sent to her athlete hubby.

    "See u soon," Alexis claimed that Angelina, 37, wrote.

    "I just want to say if you're a fan of her, that's weird. You don't message a married woman's man... weirdo," Alexis said in the clip.

    Alexis continued with other TikTok videos reacting to the situation, including another apparent message from Angelina calling Alexis' behavior childish.

    LOVE CHAOS

    Angelina, who has been accused of cheating in the past, is currently engaged to fiancé Vinny Tortorella.
