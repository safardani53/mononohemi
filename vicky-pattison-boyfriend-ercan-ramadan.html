    FORMER Geordie Shore star Vicky Pattison has been in a relationship for a number of years.

    After her engagement to her childhood sweetheart John Noble fell apart, the Celebrity SAS contestant began dating Ercan Ramadan. We've got the lowdown.

    Who is Vicky Pattinson's boyfriend Ercan Ramadan?

    Much like his partner, Ercan has been used to the nitty-gritty of reality TV.

    Hailing from Essex, and born on September 6, 1983, making him 37 years old, the former labourer and builder appeared on Towie in series 19.

    He has also been seen in the summer special, The Only Way Is Marbs.

    Although he is dating Vicky, he has had the opportunity to get chummy with other reality stars such as Love Island 2018 winner Jack Fincham.

    Both men have often been pictured enjoying nights out together on social media.

    When did he star on The Only Way Is Essex?

    As mentioned above, Ercan starred in Towie during the show's 19th instalment, back in 2016.

    That same year, he appeared in The Only Way Is Marbs, before starring in twelve episodes of the popular reality show.

    His last appearance, however, was in 2017.

    More on Vicky Pattison

    What does Ercan Ramadan do now?

    Following his TV debut, Ercan has become a popular social media personality.

    He is perhaps best known for his relationship with Vicky, which got off to a rocky start.

    After a string of dates in January 2019, only mere months after her split from John Noble, Vicky called things off, worried that the relation was "moving too quickly."

    Luckily, the break was only a few weeks. Ercan and Vicky have been going strong ever since.

    In November 2020, during an interview with Closer, Vicky opened up about their relationship.

    She said: "We're probably closer than a couple who have been together for less than two years should be, but I'm really happy with him.

    "I'm just pleased to see that the more I learn about him, the more I love him. I'm a very lucky bug."

    The pair are seemingly looking forward to their future together as they bought their own home in Essex.

    While they are in no rush to build their own family, Vicky has hinted that children could be in the horizon for them.

    Referring to her niece, she told Closer: "I am happy to be the cool aunt to baby Mavie.

    "Maybe someday I'll give her a baby cousin to play with too. What will be, will be."

    Is Ercan Ramadan on social media?

    As mentioned above, Ercan has become quite the social media star after his appearances in Towie.
