    MEGHAN King Edmonds revealed her estranged husband Jim is in a new relationship with a friend they once had a threesome with.

    The former Real Housewives of Orange County star dropped the bombshell on her podcast, Intimate Knowledge, only days after Jim deleted all of their Instagram photos together.

    Meghan, 35, explained how she found out her ex is “seeing somebody.”

    She revealed: “We had just gotten married. I knew Jim had this bad boy past and we were newlyweds and trying to have fun. He wanted to have a threesome… We decided to have a threesome - a consensual adult decision - with a friend of mine.

    “I felt very comfortable with her. It just felt like, ‘OK, if I’m going to do this, this can be with who, and I’m comfortable with that.’”

    She insisted it was a one-time thing.

    But the former Bravo star then noticed the friend drove a Range Rover and wore a Cartier watch despite not working.

    Meghan alleged: “We saw her together. We were very friendly, small talk. But he wasn’t, it was just me... Something weird between them. They almost acted like I was the one out of something.”

    When Meghan’s co-host asked if she believes he and the friend had an affair, she responded: “I think they probably had sex more than one time.”

    Meghan then revealed that Jim, 49, took his new girlfriend to Cabo and that she allegedly used his credit card to purchase items for the trip.

    She said: “They are in Cabo right now. This girl who I thought was my friend, who I cast aside my [uncomfortableness] with a threesome, everything that I had in my head about a threesome being weird and gross because I was fun and wanted to do things for my husband.

    “And then this girl betrayed me.”

    Meghan even confronted the woman over text.

    She claimed: “I texted her and said, 'I’m OK, I’m an adult.' I’m OK with people moving on, but why couldn’t you have said to me - and a friend, she’s a friend! Why couldn’t she have said something to me? Can’t she go find somebody else?

    Meghan then broke down crying: “I never told anybody I had a threesome because it just felt like something I wasn’t proud of. And now this is the woman he’s with?

    "And spending money on her when he can’t even give me the money I need while I’m supporting our three children... It’s just so hurtful.”

    Jim responded to Meghan's claims by accusing her of having affairs throughout their marriage.

    He told E! News: "I am tired of the lies for publicity. I filed for divorce over 90 days ago. Meghan has filed as well. The marriage is and has been over.

    "I was invited to a surprise birthday party this weekend for one of my best friends and decided to bring a date. There is no affair going on here."

    The baseball broadcaster continued the woman is not Meghan's friend, as they haven't spoken in over three years.

    He continued: "As far as threesomes go, there were a more than few initiated by Meghan and only Meghan. Recently, I have learned that she carried on with a few of these women without me being present.

    "Would that not being considered cheating? How can she have a podcast talking about intimacy and relationships when she has neither?"
