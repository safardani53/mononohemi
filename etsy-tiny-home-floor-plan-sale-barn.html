    HOMEOWNERS have shared rave reviews about a tiny home floor plan that is just $99.

    There are so many options for people looking to buy tiny homes.

    Handy homeowners and those with the budget to hire a contractor can create the home of their dreams by buying floor plans rather than a building or build kit.

    On Etsy, homebuilders have begun selling tiny home construction plans for spacious dwellings.

    Payton McGarvey owns an Etsy shop (PaytonMcGravey10) where he sells the floor plans of a two-bedroom, two-bath "barndominium" home and the material list.

    It is called a "barndominium" because it offers the space and comfort of a condominium with the architectural style of a barn.

    read more on homes

    The home measures 1,200 square feet and has an open floor plan with a large kitchen and living room.

    There are several windows that let in natural light throughout the home.

    The floor plans cost $99 and are extremely detailed, including plumbing and electrical plans, exterior drawings, and a foundation and anchor bolt plan.

    Once payment is confirmed the plans can be instantly downloaded to get the building process started.

    Most read in Money

    McGarvey also sells the materials list for $49.

    Buyers left rave reviews for the plans, rating it nearly five-out-of-five stars.

    "This house is exactly what I need and want. Thrilled to find it!" one person wrote.

    Another said: "Working with a contractor now to get this build started. So excited!"

    ANOTHER AFFORDABLE OPTION

    Etsy is also selling the floor plans for a two-bedroom cabin for just $82.50, marked down from $150.

    The home is built out of an 80-foot container, featuring two spacious bedrooms, a kitchen, bathroom, living room, and even a porch and terrace.

    Several floor-to-ceiling windows let in a ton of light and make the space feel much larger.

    And the terrace is big enough for an outdoor seating area to entertain guests.

    Included in the purchase is a full detailed construction plan, elevation plans, section plans, electrical plans, and drain-waste plumbing plans.

    Plus, 3D interior and exterior design renderings and a step-by-step guide to building the home.
