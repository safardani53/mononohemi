    Toya Johnson slammed DJ Akademiks for referring to her daughter Reginae Carter as a “bi*ch” and slamming her dating history. On September 24, the 31-year-old celebrity commented about Carter’s dating life on the public stage, namely, her incarcerated ex-boyfriend YFN Lucci and her new boyfriend, YouTuber Armon Warren, in a video uploaded by Akademiks.

    He went on to say that Reginae’s boyfriend is “more feminine than she is,” and that he is not a “gangsta.” Toya Johnson took the problem into her own hands when DJ Akademiks’ remarks about Carter’s love life went public, and she criticized the YouTuber for talking about her daughter.

    “I can’t tolerate males that do b**ch sh*t, Akademiks. Keep my daughter’s name from coming out of your lips. Clout chasing has reached an all-time high. Allow her to enjoy her 23-year-old life. Be concerned about yourself and your family. Thanks.”

    What did Reginae Carter say to DJ Akademiks?

    Reginae formerly dated rapper YFN Lucci, but they split up when he was arrested for murder. Akademiks chastised her for the same, saying:

    “Reginae adored hood n***as and YFN Lucci.”

    He proceeded to brag about her, claiming that when they were together, Reginae Carter attended all-star-studded events with YFN, ready to take on all that life had to offer.

    “You know this b**ch—I won’t call her a b**ch. These girls are like hood n**ga until he’s sentenced to 25 years in prison. Everything was OK with them until she noticed n***a had caught a RICO.”

    He then spent three minutes discussing Reginae Carter’s love life before bringing up the subject of her new lover, Warren. DJ Akademiks went on to say that she has “downgraded” herself and that Warren is the “opposite of gangsta,” adding that he seems “more feminine than her.” Toya Johnson, Reginae’s mother, was offended by his remarks. Johnson went on to chastise the YouTuber, saying that he shouldn’t be concerned about the 23-year-dating old’s life.

    She stated:

    “I despise it when people steal my character away from me, but one thing is that I don’t play about my children.” Stop toying with my kid, @akademiks. You’ve grown to be concerned about what a 23-year-old is doing. It’s not cool to call her a b**ch and such s**t. You guys are worse than women, and this s**t is out of control.”

    She also posted a throwback photo with Reginae Carter, expressing her love for her firstborn. Reginae was born on November 29, 1998, to Lil Wayne and Toya Johnson while the two were adolescents. They married in 2004, but their union was short-lived, and they divorced in 2006.

    Reginae was a member of the R&B female group OMG, according to Famous Birthdays. She has 6.7 million Instagram followers and is quite active on the platform. She has three half-brothers on her father’s side: Dwayne Carter III, Kameron Carter, and Neal Carter. Reginae Carter has a younger sister called Reign Ryan Rushing, whom she inherited from her mother. DJ Akademik and Reginae’s father, Lil Wayne, have yet to reply to Toya’s or the YouTuber’s contentious statements.
