    FRANKIE Sims is the gorgeous yet unknown sister of reality TV starlets Chloe and Demi Sims - though that is all set to change now as she follows in their glamorous footsteps by appearing in Towie.

    Here's what you need to know about the middle sister and blonde beauty...

    Who is Frankie Sims?

    Not much is known yet about Frankie Sims.

    She is sisters with Chloe and Demi Sims, and is 24-years-old, making her the middle sister.

    Chloe Sims is 36-years-old, and Demi Sims is 22-years-old.

    With her long blonde hair and beautiful face, she bears a striking resemblance to her two glamorous sisters.

    And with an Instagram full of selfies, it seems the budding TV star isn't shy of a pose or two.

    Frankie is also the cousin of Frankie and Joey Essex.

    The Sims sisters were born in Newbury Park, London, England.

    Last year, Frankie's sister Chloe revealed how she was abandoned by her mother at the age of three.

    Is Frankie Sims joining Towie?

    The Sun reported on 23, August, 2019 that Frankie and her two sisters were spotted filming for the upcoming series of Towie.

    This means it is likely that she will be joining her fellow Sims sisters on the show.

    Frankie wore an elegant long black dress with net detail in the snap, while Chloe opted for a green bodysuit and Demi wore a green two piece.

    Chloe shot to fame when she signed up to Towie in 2011, and has been a firm fan favourite ever since.

    Her brother Charlie enjoyed a stint between 2014 and 2015, and Demi joined the show in November last year - proving to be a roaring success.

    Based on the trailer, it looks like Demi will be back for the new series too.

    No doubt Frankie will soon become a firm favourite with fans too.

    More on Towie

    Is Demi Sims dating Megan Barton-Hanson?

    After meeting on E4's Celebs Go Dating, Frankie's sister Demi started dating Love Island's Megan Barton Hanson.

    The pair went public with their blossoming romance at the ITV summer party on July 17.

    A source explained that while they had both hoped to find love with a non-celebrity, the TV stars were instantly attracted to one another.

    However, the pair have now reportedly split following a string of jealous rows.

    When is Towie returning to ITVBe?

    There’s no official start date for Towie series 25, but with the release of a trailer, it seems that eager fans will soon have a date to mark in their diaries.

    Yup, the spanking new series will come to our screens on Sunday, September 1, 2019.

    The show’s 24th run consisted of a whopping 11 episodes, so it’s unlikely that there will be any change to the total number of weeks we’ll get to enjoy the show.
