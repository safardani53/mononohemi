    A WOMAN who turned her Ford Fiesta into a campervan using sheets of cardboard and chunks of old wood has claimed that it's really cosy inside.

    Posting on TikTok under the username Fodor Evelin, she showed viewers just how she transformed her motor.

    The TikToker dubbed her creation the "Camper Fiesta", detailing how she and her partner stripped out the inside.

    And she shocked vehicle owners with the transformation of her small Ford Fiesta into a snug retreat.

    Fodor and her partner revealed they were looking to spruce up their Fiesta to prepare for a road trip to France.

    By emptying the boot of her car and stripping out the back seats, Fodor was able to make plenty of room for two people to spend the night in.

    She even had space for a teddy bear to perch nicely on top of the comfy red and white blankets on top.
