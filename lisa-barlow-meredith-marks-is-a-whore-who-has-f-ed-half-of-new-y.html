    Meredith Marks is a Whore Who Has F---ed Half of New York!

    05 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Come on, Lisa Barlow.

    Go on and tell us how you really feel about Meredith Marks.

    Over the course of this ongoing Real Housewives of Salt Lake City season, there’s been major tension between the former close friends.

    But it sure looks (or sounds?) as if Barlow will take things to an entirely new and heated level on the January 30 episode of the popular Bravo reality show.

    Toward the end of a network preview for the upcoming installment, Barlow is shown trashing Meredith off-camera… seemingly unaware that her microphone is picking up the whole exchange.

    The whole pointed and very personal exchange, we should note.

    "Meredith can go f— herself. I’m done with her," Barlow says.

    "I f—ing hate her. She’s a whore. She’s f—ed half of New York!"

    Well… okay then!

    Based on this same video, it seems the impressive outburst may have been prompted by Marks refusing to take Barlow’s side during a separate intense moment with Mary Cosby.

    We won’t know for certain what this is all about until Sunday night, although Barlow did appear on Watch What Happens Live two days ago and touched on the footage during her appearance.

    (It’s almost as though this has all been planned and scripted, huh?)

    Asked why she thought Meredith deleted the birthday post she shared in celebration of Barlow back last month, Lisa responded:

    "It’s her profile, she can do whatever she wants with it. I’m sure it’s based on the next episode. I have a little rant that I’m not proud of, but I hit my boiling point."

    Back on December 14, Marks wrote on Instagram of her then-tight pal:

    "Happy birthday to my Sag sister! I’m so grateful for all of our memories! Have the best celebration."

    Earlier this month, meanwhile, Andy Cohen teased that some major drama was on its way to Utah.

    "The women saw the remaining six or seven episodes before the reunion, and there’s something coming up that is jaw-dropping that no one knows about that will rock the foundation of two of the Housewives," he said on his Radio Andy show, adding at the time:

    "When you see this play out and you realize that this was shown to the women the day before they shot the reunion and then they had to deal with it at the reunion, you will be quite engaged."

    Again: it’s almost as if producers couldn’t have scripted things any better!

    Such has been the case all season long, of course, when it comes to The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City.

    There’s been the constant presence of Jen Shah, who was arrested last March for her alleged role in a telemarketing scheme that may have defrauded a countless number of senior citizens.

    And there’s now a significant controversy around Jennie Nguyen, whose old anti-Black Lives Matter social media posts recently resurfaced online.

    Nguyen has apologized for her what she said in 2020, but some of her colleagues clearly aren’t satisfied.

    "I am sickened by my co-worker’s racist and prejudicial posts that recently surfaced,” Marks wrote in response, for example.

    “I do not stand behind or support these posts in any way, shape or form. I stand with and support the communities affected and targeted by this.

    "I am deeply disturbed and outraged by her actions. Black Lives Matter.”
