    15 Apr 2024, 00:00

    CHRISTMAS Day is for turkey and cranberry, finishing off the selection boxes - and slumping on the sofa watching the TV.

    Whether that's drama-filled soaps, fun children's movies, tense British dramas or your favourite quizzes given a festive twist, we have the full BBC, ITV and Channel 4 schedule... what will you be picking?

    The BBC are focusing on their most popular family favourites such as Strictly Come Dancing, Call The Midwife, and the highly-anticipated return of Gavin And Stacey.

    ITV meanwhile are going heavy on the brain teasers with Christmas specials of The Chase, Tipping Point, and Who Wants To Be A Millionaire.

    Channel 5 meanwhile will have a brand new episode of The Two Ronnies: The Unseen Sketches.

    Of course if neither of these tickle your fancy you could always go for an online streaming service.
