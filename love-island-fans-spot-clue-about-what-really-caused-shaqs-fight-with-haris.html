    Love Island fans spot clue about what really caused Shaqs fight with Haris

    LOVE Island has only been on our screens for less than a week but already tempers have flared. So much so, things nearly came to blows during last night's episode between Shaq Muhammad and Haris Namani - and fans are convinced they know the reason behind the bust up.

    04 Feb 2024, 00:00

    LOVE Island has only been on our screens for less than a week but already tempers have flared.

    So much so, things nearly came to blows during last night's episode between Shaq Muhammad and Haris Namani - and fans are convinced they know the reason behind the bust up.

    Although viewers believed the fight occurred when Haris asked newcomer David Salako about the kiss he shared with Shaq's love interest Tanya - fans are sure the villa's yellow bean bag was to blame.

    Yes you read that correctly. According to Love Island fans the yellow bean bag is cursed and has been the root of all evil in the past.

    One fan said: " Tanya sat on the yellow beanbag and suddenly Shaq got into a fight. The beanbag is still cursed."

    Another fan said: "Has the curse of the yellow beanbag come back, what's going on?"

    Historically, Love Island fans have blamed this particular piece of furniture for causing strife in the villa.

    During last year's series, contestants wouldn't go anywhere near it due to its dark powers.

    At the time, it was believed anyone who sat down on it was bound to attract trouble.

    Voice over star Iain Stirling said at the time of Davide's outburst: "An exclusive for Unseen Bits - I can now reveal what caused this Italian Stallion to bolt."

    Most read in Love Island

    He added: "No one escapes the curse of the yellow beanbag."

    And aside from Iain, his sentiments were also shared by Love Island's, Coco Lodge. She said: "That yellow beanbag causes drama - anyone who sits on it, you get drama."

    And it seems fans think farmer Will Young has fallen victim of the curse.

    Within minutes of arriving at the South African villa Will sat on the offending beanbag.

    And this, it seems, explains why he was the only boy left standing when couples were chosen.

    Also it explains why he messed up what little chance he had with Olivia Hawkins when he revealed he had a tattoo of an ex girlfriend.
